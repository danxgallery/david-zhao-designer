App Design - Wayfinder

UI / UX Design

August 2018 - November 2018

Summary

The Wayfinder project was a school project in RIT. We were broken into different cooperating teams. The client was Rochester Regional Health (RRH).

They wanted us to create an Augmented Reality that can help both Patients and Coworkers navigate the Hospital in a simple way. I was in a team that dealt with language barriers.

Prototype

Goal

Control Efficiency of the Hospital

People can get lost very easily. Especially when an emergency is going on. This can cause a traffic jam for the patients and doctors to show up in the right room on time.

Improve Localization

Some people do not understand English very well or do not understand it at all. We would like to added this feature to break the language barrier between locals and doctors, and doctors to doctors.

Improve Emotion Experience

Hospital is a controlled environment. But things can be very stressful quickly. The app is develop to reduce stress and make people calmer.
